ListToBid Auction Policy

1. Sales Finality
   - All auction sales are final unless the item is materially misrepresented.
   - The 5% ListToBid platform fee is non-refundable under all circumstances.

2. Seller Obligations
   - Sellers must complete Know Your Customer (KYC) and Know Your Business (KYB) verification before listing.
   - One verified bank account must be on file for payouts and refunds.
   - Sellers are responsible for covering taxes, applicable payment processor fees, and shipping costs if ListToBid’s shipping account is used.

3. Buyer Obligations
   - Buyers must validate payment methods before bidding.
   - Winning bids are binding and must be honored.

4. Refunds
   - Refunds are not supported unless fraud or misrepresentation is proven.
   - In such cases, refunds are merchant-funded and must include product price, taxes, processor fees, and shipping costs if applicable.
   - ListToBid does not issue refunds from its own funds.

5. Crypto Transactions
   - Sellers may accept crypto payments only through approved gateways (e.g., Coinbase Commerce, BitPay).
   - Refunds for crypto transactions are merchant-funded and must be issued in the same currency or fiat equivalent.
   - All crypto transactions are subject to AML/KYC compliance requirements.
   - The 5% ListToBid platform fee is non-refundable regardless of payment type.

6. AML Compliance
   - ListToBid complies with U.S. AML regulations under the Bank Secrecy Act.
   - Transactions of $10,000 or more in crypto or fiat equivalents will be reported as required.
   - Transactions of $9,000 or more may be flagged for review to prevent structuring.
