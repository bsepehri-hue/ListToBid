AML Compliance
- ListToBid complies with U.S. AML regulations under the Bank Secrecy Act.
- Transactions of $10,000 or more in cash (or equivalent) will be reported to the IRS via Form 8300.
- Transactions of $9,000 or more may be flagged for review to prevent structuring.
- Merchants must complete KYC/KYB verification before storefront activation.
- ListToBid does not issue refunds or credits from its own funds; all
 merchant obligations remain in force.

Crypto Payments
- ListToBid does not transact in cash. All merchants must maintain one verified bank account on file.
- Merchants may accept crypto payments through approved gateways (e.g., Coinbase Commerce, BitPay).
- Refunds for crypto transactions are merchant-funded and must be issued in the same currency or fiat equivalent.
- Merchants remain responsible for covering taxes, applicable fees, and shipping costs.
- The 5% ListToBid platform fee is non-refundable regardless of payment type.
- All crypto transactions are subject to AML/KYC compliance requirements.

Crypto Transactions
- Merchants may accept crypto payments only through approved gateways.
- All merchants must maintain one verified bank account on file for payouts and refunds.
- Refunds are merchant-funded and must include product price, taxes, gateway fees, and shipping costs if applicable.
- Refunds may be issued in the same crypto currency or fiat equivalent.
- The 5% ListToBid platform fee is non-refundable.
- All crypto transactions are subject to AML/KYC compliance requirements.

____________________________

Onboarding Fee
- All merchants are subject to a one-time $99 onboarding fee.
- The fee is non-refundable and covers expenses related to merchant verification, compliance checks, and storefront activation.
- The onboarding fee is separate from transaction fees and platform commissions.

_______________________

Merchant Verification
- All merchants must provide a valid Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN).
- Accepted forms of TIN include SSN, EIN, or ITIN.
- Sole proprietors may use EIN or SSN; individuals without SSNs may use ITIN.
- One verified bank account must be on file for payouts and refunds.
- ListToBid does not store SSNs or ITINs directly; verification is handled securely through approved gateways.
