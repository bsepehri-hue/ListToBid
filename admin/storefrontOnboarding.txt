Onboarding Canon Summary

5-Step Flow

Personal & Business Information

Collect essentials: name, business name, contact info, address.

Establish identity and credibility.

Managed Services & Products

Present services as product cards with images, descriptions, and benefits.

Examples: Phone system, Domain + Email, Stripe/Square POS terminals, UPS/FedEx shipping integration, Custom Automation.

Each product card opens a modal with details and a checkmark option.

Shopping cart style: merchants select services, $99 onboarding fee is default.

Terms, Policies & Signing

Display terms & conditions and privacy policy in plain language.

Merchant checks agreement box.

Redirect to shopping cart for verification.

$99 onboarding fee locked in as default.

Cart doubles as a mini demo, showing selected services before payment.

Storefront Setup

Upload logo and banner.

Choose template or layout style.

Real-time preview of storefront.

Google Review Prompt

Ceremonial confirmation: "Your Storefront is Live!"

Prompt for 5-star Google review.

Incentive: token credit, discount, or referral perk.

Shopping Cart Canon

Default $99 Fee → always pre-selected, framed as activation seal.

Managed Services as Products → POS terminals, phone system, domain/email, shipping integration, custom automation.

Visual Cart Summary → images, descriptions, checkmarks, running total.

Vendor Routing → each checked item auto-routed to vendor for activation.

Custom Automation Canon

Marketing flows (social posts, discounts).
